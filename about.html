About Us

FrameFlash Productions is built by a group of people who share the same love for cinema. Each of us brings something different — whether it’s storytelling, visuals, music, or design — and that mix is what gives our work its character. More than just a team, we’re a bunch of creators who believe in making films that stay with people

Jayanth - Founder, Director & Creator The one who started it all, bringing ideas to life and steering every project from vision to execution. Always pushing for something fresh, with an eye on the bigger picture.

Sampath - Co-Founder & Cinematographer Handles the camera with ease, turning simple shots into frames that carry depth and mood. Focused on details that often go unnoticed but make all the difference.

Rhythm Nandi - Graphic Designer Gives FrameFlash its visual style through clean and striking designs. From posters to graphics, adds the layer of polish that ties everything together.

Rahul Madeti - Music Director Adds soul to our work through sound. The kind of music that doesn’t just play in the background but becomes part of the story itself.

Lalith Reddy - Editor Takes all the pieces and brings them together into one smooth flow. Sharp with storytelling, making sure the final cut feels natural and engaging.

Vyshrawan - Website Designer Builds the online home for FrameFlash. Makes sure everything looks neat, accessible, and represents the team in the best possible way.
