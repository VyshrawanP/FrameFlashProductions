Our Projects

Cipher is a gripping new mystery from FrameFlash Productions, where “the silence cracked, the pattern bled, and the cipher unlocked.” Directed and encoded by Jayanth, with a story by Vinay and an original score by Rahul Madeti, this project brings together an exciting cast — Anish and Steve Reagen — under the sharp cinematography of Sampath, precise editing by Lalith, and striking graphics by Rhythm. A true FrameFlash Productions Original, Cipher invites you to decode its secrets and step inside a world where every clue matters. Follow us on Instagram @frameflash_productions and join the challenge: #Cipher #FrameFlashProductions #DecodeItIfYouCan.
GREY is a heartfelt new short from FrameFlash Productions — an intimate journey into life’s quiet contrasts. Written and directed by Jayanth, from a story by K Venu, the film stars Arpit Patel and Lalith Chandra, with moving performances supported by K Venu and Hrishi Koushal. Visual storytelling comes alive through the cinematography of Amrith Ashok, while Mitesh Sai Devar brings precision to editing and SFX. Stunning graphics by Rhythm complete the experience. A true FrameFlash Productions Original, GREY invites you to discover the colours hidden within the shade.
