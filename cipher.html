CIPHER

Cipher is a gripping new mystery from FrameFlash Productions, where “the silence cracked, the pattern bled, and the cipher unlocked.” Directed and encoded by Jayanth, with a story by Vinay and an original score by Rahul Madeti, this project brings together an exciting cast — Anish and Steve Reagen — under the sharp cinematography of Sampath, precise editing by Lalith, and striking graphics by Rhythm. A true FrameFlash Productions Original, Cipher invites you to decode its secrets and step inside a world where every clue matters.
